This is your Music Bubble

This bubble will reside on every page you visit, providing handy access to common controls for Google Music.

When it's grey it's not connected to Google Music. In another tab, open Google Music, or refresh the page if it's already running. Start playing some music then come back here for the rest of the tutorial.

You can open a new tab with Google Music by double clicking on the Bubble when it's grey! Try it!

Connected

When the bubble is orange you are connected to Google Music and in control of the player.

Move your mouse over the bubble to expand the controls.

Control

Press the buttons to control Google Music. Click around. Make sure you see what everything does.

  • If music is playing, hovering over the bubbles will show the song title. You can even rate the song with the click of a button. Handy!
  • Double clicking the main button will activate the Google Music tab for full control.
  • Scrolling up and down will adjust volume.

Now try doing a long click over the main bubble. About half a second should do it.

Tip: You can use the browser back button to bo back in the tutorial if you need to.

Customize

You can put the bubble wherever you want! Click the move button and drag it it around to wherever you want it.

Try moving it to the bottom right corner.
If you want to stop the bubble from showing up on certain sites press the blacklist button in the bottom right of the main bubble.

You can remove them later from the options page.

Done!

The bubble will now be there while you're browsing. Of course, you can move the bubble around any time.

That's it.

Opened tabs will need to be refreshed before the bubble shows up.
